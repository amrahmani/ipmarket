Roadmap & recommended MVP

  1. Seed curated inventory in one niche (e.g. Energy) and invite trusted sellers.
  2. Provide AI lay summaries and auto-tags to improve discoverability.
  3. Manual matchmaking and early brokerage support to create reference deals.
  4. Monetise with paid featured listings, editorial summaries, and success commissions.

This roadmap mirrors the feasibility analysis uploaded with this demo (used to design the site flow and content).

Trust & legal (summary)

A marketplace for IP must incorporate verification, sample NDA flows, and clear terms to reduce transaction friction. Use manual vetting in early stages and partner with trusted technology transfer offices.
